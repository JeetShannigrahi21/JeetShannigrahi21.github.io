About

I am a graduate student in Physics at the University of British Columbia (Stewart Blusson Quantum Matter Institute). My current work focuses on the finite-temperature properties of the Holstein polaron, including the temperature evolution of the effective mass and lifetime, across one-, two-, and three-dimensional systems.
